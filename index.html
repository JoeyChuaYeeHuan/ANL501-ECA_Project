Featured Project: ANL501 - Data Visualization & Storytelling

📍 The Legacy of Civil War: Landmines, ERWs, and Their Effects on Cambodian Communities

This project explores the impact of landmines and explosive remnants of war (ERWs) in Cambodia, using data visualization and geospatial analysis. I leveraged **R, GIS, and RMarkdown** to create meaningful insights and policy recommendations based on historical landmine data.

🔹 Key Technologies Used:

  • R (ggplot2, dplyr, sf, tidyverse)
  • RMarkdown for reproducible reporting
  • GIS & Spatial Data Visualization
